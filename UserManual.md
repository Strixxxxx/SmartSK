# SmartSK User Manual

This manual provides a comprehensive guide to using the SmartSK platform. It is divided into three sections: Public Access, Client (User) Functionality, and Administrator Functionality.

---

## Part 1: Public Access

This section covers all features available to the general public without needing to log in.

### Homepage (Project Portfolio)
The homepage of SmartSK showcases a portfolio of recent and ongoing projects. Each project is displayed with its title and a brief description, allowing visitors to get a quick overview of the Sangguniang Kabataan's activities.

### Project List
For a more detailed view, the "Project List" page provides a comprehensive list of all public projects. This view is ideal for finding specific projects or seeing a complete record of initiatives.

### Logging In
To access user-specific features, you must log in.
1.  From the homepage, initiate the login process.
2.  You will first be prompted to select your Barangay (e.g., San Bartolome or Nagkaisang Nayon).
3.  After selecting your Barangay, you will be directed to a login screen to enter your username/email and password.

### Forgot Password
If you have forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. You will be guided through a process to recover your account using your username or email address.

---

## Part 2: Client (SK Official) Functionality

This section is for logged-in SK Officials and covers the features available on the client-side dashboard.

### Dashboard
After logging in, you will land on your personal dashboard. This page serves as your main hub, displaying a feed of recent project posts from your community.

### Creating a Project Post
From your dashboard, you can share updates about your projects with the public.
- Click the **"Create Post"** button.
- A window will appear allowing you to enter a **Title**, **Description**, and **attach files** (images or videos) for your post.

### My Projects
This section allows you to manage your project proposals.
- **Submit Proposals:** You can submit new project proposals for review.
- **View Status:** Track the status of your submitted proposals (e.g., Pending Review, Revision Requested, Approved, Rejected).
- **Review Remarks:** View feedback and comments from the SK Chairperson or Administrators who have reviewed your proposal.

### Forecast
The Forecast page provides powerful analytics based on historical data. You can visualize project budget allocations over the years, grouped by different criteria such as the responsible committee or project category. This helps in understanding past trends and planning for the future.

### Predictive Analytics
This advanced feature uses AI to provide insights for future projects. Based on historical data and current trends, it can help you:
- Identify potential new project ideas.
- Understand key success factors.
- Anticipate risks and develop mitigation strategies.

## Part 3: Administrator Functionality

This section is for users with administrative privileges (e.g., SK Chairperson, Master Admin) and details the features available in the Admin Panel.

### Admin Dashboard
The Admin Dashboard is the central control panel. It provides a high-level overview of system activity, user statistics, and quick links to all administrative functions.

### Account Creation
This module allows administrators to create and manage user accounts for SK Officials within their barangay.

### Roles Management
Administrators can assign specific roles (e.g., SK Official, SK Chairperson, System Admin) to users, controlling their level of access and permissions within the system.

### Projects (Admin View)
This section is for reviewing and managing all project proposals submitted by users. Admins can:
- View all submitted projects.
- Approve or reject proposals.
- Request revisions and provide feedback to the user.

### Raw Data Management
This critical feature allows admins to upload and manage the system's raw historical data via CSV files. This data is the foundation for the Forecast and Predictive Analytics modules.

### Session Log
For security and monitoring, the Session Log provides a complete record of all user login and logout activities, including timestamps.

### Audit Trail
The Audit Trail is a detailed, chronological log that tracks every significant action performed within the system. This includes creating accounts, updating projects, changing roles, and more, providing a comprehensive record for accountability.

### Archive
Administrators can use this module to archive old projects and user accounts, removing them from active lists while retaining them in the database. Items can also be restored from the archive if needed.

### Backup and Restore
This powerful feature allows admins to safeguard the system's data.
- **Backup:** Create a complete backup of the application database.
- **Restore:** Restore the system to a previous state using a backup file.
